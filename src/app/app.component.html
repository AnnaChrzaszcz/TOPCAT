Dance Battle 21.7
15:00
Dear all! Get ready for one of the biggest attractions of the evening! Apart from the fierce matches and the best style competition, each team will be challenged to participate in the DANCE BATTLE! What is this about? By the end of next week please send us two songs to which you will design a choreography. Each team will have to present their dance to the audience. During the tournament, we will draw you a rival against whom you will fight on stage. Also, on Saturday morning, each team will draw a theme that you and your rival will have to spontaneously implement into your choreography (the rival will have the same theme), for example: a belly dance, jungle dance, baptism, etc. You will have all day to think about your performance. The winning teams will move on to the next stage, and with every stage it will be more and more difficult! In the second round, the second song of your choice will be played, but the theme will be drawn right before the performance itself. In the finals, there will be 3 teams and you will all dance together to our song. Good luck!
